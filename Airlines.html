World's Best Airlines – Skytrax Ranking

1. Qatar Airways

Qatar Airways, based in Doha, is recognized for its Qsuite business class, A350 fleet, world-class catering, and refined onboard experience. It operates to over 160 destinations and is part of the oneworld alliance. Its hub, Hamad International Airport, is among the best globally.

2. Singapore Airlines

Singapore Airlines stands out for its hospitality, KrisWorld entertainment, and top-tier service across all classes. Operating from Changi Airport, it runs a modern fleet including the A350, Boeing 787-10, and A380, with a global reputation for safety and punctuality.

3. Emirates

Emirates, based in Dubai, is famous for its ICE inflight entertainment, luxurious first-class suites, and vast long-haul route network. It operates the largest A380 fleet and connects over 150 destinations worldwide.

4. ANA All Nippon Airways

ANA is Japan’s largest airline and a member of Star Alliance. Known for its precision and service excellence, it offers clean, quiet cabins and premium offerings like "The Room" business class. ANA serves both domestic and international markets with Boeing 787 and A380 aircraft.

5. Cathay Pacific Airways

Based in Hong Kong, Cathay Pacific is known for its calm service, strong safety record, and modern long-haul fleet. It connects Asia to North America and Europe using Airbus A350s and Boeing 777-300ERs, and offers stylish lounges at its home base.

6. Japan Airlines

JAL combines traditional Japanese hospitality with world-class service. Its Sky Suite business class is highly rated, and the airline is a leader in punctuality and transpacific comfort, especially from Tokyo Haneda and Narita airports.

7. Turkish Airlines

Flying to more countries than any other airline, Turkish Airlines is headquartered in Istanbul and is renowned for its inflight catering, onboard comfort, and lounge experience. Its global network includes Europe, Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

8. EVA Air

Based in Taipei, EVA Air is known for its safety, cabin hygiene, and excellent premium economy. It connects Asia and North America with Boeing 777-300ER aircraft and is a Star Alliance member.

9. Air France

Air France has elevated its onboard service in recent years, especially in business class. It is the national carrier of France, serving Europe, North America, and Africa with Airbus A350 and Boeing 787 aircraft. It is part of the SkyTeam alliance.

10. SWISS International Air Lines

SWISS operates with efficiency and elegance. Based in Zurich, it offers premium short-haul and long-haul service and is well-known for punctuality and customer satisfaction. Its fleet includes A220, A320neo, A330, and Boeing 777 aircraft.

Source: Skytrax World Airline Awards 2024
