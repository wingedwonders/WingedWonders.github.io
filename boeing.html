Boeing 747

Released: February 9, 1969

Speed: 540 mph

Capacity: 416–660 passengers

Engines (Earlier Models): JT9D, CF6, or RB211

Engines (Latest Models): GEnx-2B67

Facts It is called Jumbo Jet or Queen of the Skies. It is a wideboody aircraft designed for mid-to-long range flights.

The Boeing 747-200, also called the “Jumbo Jet” or “Queen of the skies”, was invented on February 9th, 1969, which is now one of the most successful aircrafts, capable of mid and long-range travel. It can carry from 416-660 passengers depending on the model. The early 747 models had 4 JT9D, CF6, or RB211 engines. And the latest (747-8) has 4 GEnx-2B67 engines.

Boeing 777

Released: June 12, 1994

Speed: 560 mph

Capacity: 314–396 passengers

Engines: Three Pratt & Whitney JT8D turbofan

Facts: The Boeing 777 is known for it's efficiency and comfort. It is a widebdy aircraft designed for long-range flights

The Boeing 777-200 had finished development in 1995. It is a long-range widebody with twin engines. The 777 has an average cruising speed of around 560 mph and could carry up to 296 passengers. The Boeing 777 is known for its efficiency and passenger comfort. It has Pratt & Whitney PW4000, General Electric GE90, Rolls-Royce Trent 800, and GE9X Engines.

Boeing 787

Released: December 15, 2009

Speed: 567 mph

Capacity: 242–330 passengers

Engines: Rolls-Royce Trent 1000 and General Electric GEnx-1B.

Facts: It is celebrated for its environmental efficiency and quieter engines. It is a widebody aircraft suitable for short-tomid range flights.

The Boeing 787-8 was launched in 2011 as a wide body twin engine jet airliner designed with far better and more modern materials than previous planes. It is designed to fly from short to mid-haul routes. It can typically hold a capacity of 330 passengers. It is celebrated for its environmental efficiency, its quieter engines, and innovative design. It has Rolls-Royce Trent 1000 and General Electric GEnx-1B engines.

Boeing 737

Released: April 9, 1967

Speed: 540 mph

Capacity: 85–230 passengers

Engines (Earlier Models): JT8D engines

Engines (Latest Models): CFM LEAP-1B

Facts: It is globally known as the best-selling plane globally due to its versatility and reliability. It is a Narrow body short-to-mid range aircraft.

The Boeing 737-100 was released in 1967, which has now become one of the worlds most widely used aircraft currently. It is a narrow body plane, With a cruising speed of up to 540 mph, with a range of 7,730 miles. It is globally known as the best-selling plane globally due to its versatility and reliability. It has 2 JT8D engines, with the latest engines on the Boeing 737 MAXs are CFM LEAP-1B engines.
